About Golden Bean

Welcome to Golden Bean, your neighborhood coffee haven! Since our founding in 2020, we have been passionate about serving the finest Arabica blends and creating a cozy space for coffee lovers to connect, relax, and enjoy. Our beans are ethically sourced, roasted to perfection, and brewed with love by our expert baristas.

Whether you’re here for your morning pick-me-up, a sweet treat, or a friendly chat, Golden Bean is dedicated to making every visit special. Thank you for being part of our coffee family!
